What's Happening?

The proposed Recovery and Wellness Campus at 800 W Mission Road is moving forward without proper community input, despite its proximity to several schools and neighborhoods.

We are asking for:

  • A pause in the project until full community consultation
  • A formal public hearing
  • An independent safety and environmental impact assessment
  • Support for relocation, downscaling, or reconfiguration
